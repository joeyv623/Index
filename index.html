Freezer Breakfast Burritos
🍳 Breakfast⏱ 1–2 hours🌡 Stovetop🥦 Kid-Friendly👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 5–6, 7+
Make 12–16 burritos at once, wrap and freeze. Microwave 2 min for a hot school-morning breakfast.
🛒 Key Ingredients
Large flour tortillas📦Eggs📦Breakfast sausage📦Cheddar cheese📦Bell pepper📦Onion📦Salsa📦
1
Scramble eggs with sautéed sausage, peppers, and onion. Season well.
2
Lay out tortillas. Layer egg mixture, cheese, and salsa.
3
Roll tightly, wrap in foil, and freeze. Label with date.
